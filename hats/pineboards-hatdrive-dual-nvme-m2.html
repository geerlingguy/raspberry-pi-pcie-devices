Description

Pineboards’ HatDrive! Dual NVMe Board includes an ASmedia ASM1182e PCIe Gen 2 switch chip to split one lane of PCIe Gen 2 from the Pi 5 to two 2230 or 2242 M.2 NVMe SSD M-key slots.
