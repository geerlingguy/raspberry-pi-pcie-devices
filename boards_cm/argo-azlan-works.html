Description

Argo is an opensource CM5 carrier board that fits within the same compute module footprint whilst delivering tons of features, including onboard battery management and USB-C 3.0!

2x 100pin Hirose Connectors 2x MIPI-DSI/CSI 1x Micro-HDMI 1x HDMI FPC connector for external break out 1x PCIe FPC connector, Pi 5 compatible 2x USB-C (One for power/flashing - USB 2.0 and one regular USB 3.0) Integrated BMS (BQ25895) with up to 5A charging dependent on battery used. TPS61022 5V boost for upto 3A of constant power from battery or USB. I2C control for BQ25895 with ADC battery level monitoring. Onboard LSM6DSV16XTR IMU via I2C
