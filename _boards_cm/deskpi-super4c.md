The [DeskPi Super4c](https://deskpi.com/products/deskpi-super4c-raspberry-pi-cm5-cluster-mini-itx-board-esp32-wroom-32e-n4-module-supports-2-4ghz-wi-fi-bluetooth-power-supply-included) is a 4-node Pi CM5 Cluster Board.

It includes remote management via ESP32 (with WiFi or (future-enablement) 10/100 Ethernet), redundant 19V power input, 2 Ethernet ports per Pi (1x 2.5 Gbps, 1x 1 Gbps), and it breaks out almost all the CM5 interfaces for each node, including dual CSI/DSI display/camera, GPIO, RTC, Fan, microSD, M.2 NVMe, USB 3, USB-C (2.0), and micro HDMI.

Read more on the [DeskPi Super4C Wiki](https://wiki.deskpi.com/super4c/).
